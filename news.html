Our Story

We, Ion Electrical & Automation Company Ltd (IEACL) are one of the streamlined Electrical & Automation Companies based on Dhaka working across Bangladesh. IEACL is committed to provide reliable & sustainable solutions to be an inevitable part of the industry with our presence in every relevant sector of the local economy. IEACL is offering a range of turnkey & specialist electrical contracting work covering Engineering, procurement, construction, commissioning, operation & maintenance.

IEACL is known to customers for the highest quality workmanship while maintaining professionalism. IEACL believe in integrity, commitment, teamwork & safety. IEACL is promised to adapt changes in technology, vision & participation in time. Perfect blend technology and professional management are our strength. IEACL recognize that the key to running a successful business is understanding the clients needs and meeting their expectations.
